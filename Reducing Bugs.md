Most bugs arise from incorrect state[(1)](https://youtu.be/ShSGHb65f3M?t=2444).
Bugs are states that are not part of the specification of the program.
If you reduce the set of all possible states that the program can be in then you reduce the risk of bugs.
If you reduce the fractions of all possible states that are invalid states then you reduce the risk of bugs.
A goal is to make all states valid by construction.
